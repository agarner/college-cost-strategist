📋 Strategic Reminders

  • Schools that "meet 100% of demonstrated need" are not the same as schools that give the most free money—look at how they define need and the grant vs. loan split.
  • Merit aid is usually tied to GPA maintenance. Understand the renewal requirements before committing.
  • Early Decision is binding and limits leverage. Early Action preserves options.

🤝Negotiation Playbook

📣 Negotiation Tips

  • Always be polite and express genuine interest in attending — schools help students they want to enroll.
  • Cite a competing offer from a comparable school as leverage. Provide the award letter as documentation.
  • Ask specifically: "Is there any additional institutional grant or scholarship available given our circumstances?"
  • Appeal through the financial aid office — not admissions — after you have an offer in hand.
  • If circumstances changed (job loss, medical bills, divorce), file a Professional Judgment request with supporting documents.
